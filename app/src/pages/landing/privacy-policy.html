How We Use Your Information

We use personal information in the file we maintain about you, and other information we obtain from your current and past activities on the Application, to provide to you the services offered by the Application; resolve service and billing disputes; troubleshoot problems; bill any amounts due from you; measure consumer interest in our products and services; inform you about online and offline offers, products, services, events and updates; deliver information to you that, in some cases, is relevant to your interests, such as product news; customize your experience; detect and protect us against error, fraud and other criminal activity; enforce our Terms of Use; provide you with system or administrative messages, and as otherwise described to you at the time of collection.

We may also use personal information about you to improve our marketing and promotional efforts, to analyze Application usage, to improve our content and product offerings, and to customize the Application's content, layout, and services. These uses improve the Application and better tailor it to meet your needs, so as to provide you with a smooth, efficient, safe and customized experience while using the Application.

On occasion we use email address or other contact information to contact our Users with our newsletter or other similar communication, or to ask them for their input on our services. In general, any email or other electronic message we send you will contain a link or other instructions to permit you to unsubscribe from such email or messages, except that certain emails or messages are essential to the operation of the Application and you must cancel your account to opt out of receiving them.

What Information Do We Collect?

Our primary purpose in collecting personal information from you is to provide you with a safe, smooth, efficient, and customized experience. This allows us to provide services and features that most likely meet your needs, and to customize our service to make your experience safer and easier. We only collect personal information about you that we consider necessary for achieving this purpose.

Once you become a registered User, we require you to provide various contact and identity information, billing information, and other personal information as indicated on the relevant forms in the Application (which vary, depending on what kind of User you are), and you are no longer anonymous to us. Where possible, on these forms we indicate which fields are required and which fields are optional. We collect information when you register to join us by logging into an account that you already have with certain third-party sites ("TPS") (including, but not limited to, Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram and Twitter). For example, our Application allows you to create an account and login for the Application using your Facebook account credentials. If you are not currently registered as a user of our Application and you click on “Sign Up with Facebook,” you will first be asked to enter your Facebook credentials and then be given the option to register and join us. If you decide to create your user account by logging into a TPS account via the Application, you are using the personal information you have already provided to the TPS (such as your “real” name, email address and other information you make publicly available via the TPS) to create your user account with us. The information we collect may depend on the privacy settings you have with the TPS, so please consult the TPS’s privacy and data practices. The personal information we may collect in this way may include, but is not limited to, your name, email address, TPS sign-in or username, profile photo, and any biography you have provided to them.

In addition, as you use the Application, you can from time to time enter or send to us personal information. You always have the option to not provide information by choosing not to become a User or by not using the particular feature of the Application for which the information is being collected.

We use cookies and log data in various ways, as described below. We use cookies for two purposes. First, we use “persistent” cookies to save your login information for future logins to the Application. Second, we use “session ID” cookies to enable certain features of the Application, to better understand how you interact with the Application and to monitor aggregate usage by our users and web traffic routing on the Application. Unlike persistent cookies, session cookies are deleted from your computer when you log off from the Application and close your browser. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our Application.

When you use our Application, whether as a registered user or a non-registered user, our servers automatically record information that your browser sends (“log data”). This log data may include information such as the browser type or the webpage you were visiting before you came to our Application, pages of our Application that you visit, the time spent on those pages, information you search for on our Application, access times and dates, and other statistics. We use this information to monitor and analyze use of the Application and the Application’s technical administration, to increase the Application’s functionality and user-friendliness, and to better tailor our Application to our visitors’ needs. For example, some of this information is collected so that when you visit the Application again, it will recognize you and the information could then be used to serve advertisements and other information appropriate to your interests. We also use this information to verify that visitors to the Application meet the criteria required to process their requests.

We also use Google, as a third party vendor, to serve our ads on other websites. Google uses what it calls a “DART cookie” to enable it to serve ads to users based on their visits to participating websites. As a result of our use of Google as a vendor and the use of DART cookies, the ads that you will be served on the internet may in some cases be based on your visit to our Application.

We may also use other third-party services to assist us in using and analyzing these tools and the data they provide, and in monitoring your use of the Application.

Following your registration on the Application, you can review and change your information in your account area. If you disclose personal information in any posting in our forum or blog and wish to have it removed, please contact us at the support contact information listed below, and posted on the Application.

Our Disclosure of Your Information

We will share your personal information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy policy. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. The following describes some of the ways that your information may be disclosed in the normal scope of business to provide our services:

Anonymized Aggregated Data.

We aggregate and anonymize Application use information and disclose such information in a non-personally identifiable manner to Users and others. However, in these situations, we do not disclose any information that could be used to identify you personally.

Public Display or Display to other Users.

If you post on our forum or blog your username and other information you include is displayed in your postings or comments, and is therefore available to the public. All of your activities in the public areas of the forum will be identifiable to your User ID, and other people can see your published profile content. When you register through the Application and submit personal information to create a User profile or a collection, other users will see your name. If you choose to provide additional personal information in your profile, other users will see that additional personal information, including, but not limited to, your photo, location, website and biography. Providing additional personal information is voluntary. We recommend that you avoid disclosing any information you consider sensitive and confidential. In certain cases the Application allows Users to provide ratings and reviews of other Users, and such information will be published on the areas of the Application visible to registered Users. When completing and Evaluation Survey, comments designated as Public may be visible to all members of the public, regardless of whether they are registered users or not. Regarding comments designated as Private, we make reasonable efforts to maintain the confidentiality of these comments although we make not warrant of guarantee that these comments might not be made inadvertently public.

For example, we may use the services of third-party hosting companies to host the operation of the Application. This may involve the hosting of data, including personal information, on servers operated by those hosting companies, and in some cases those servers may be located in, or data enroute to or from those servers may transit, other jurisdictions, including the United States.
